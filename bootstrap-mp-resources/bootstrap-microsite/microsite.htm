What is Animal Crossing?

"Animal Crossing: New Horizons" is a relaxing life simulation game where players escape to a deserted island to create their own paradise. They can fish, catch bugs, gather resources, and build and customize their island home, all while interacting with anthropomorphic animal villagers and experiencing the joys of daily life in a charming virtual world.
